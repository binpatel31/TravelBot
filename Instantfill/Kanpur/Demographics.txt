
As per the provisional results of 2011 census, Kanpur city has a population of 2,767,031. The literacy rate was 84.14 per cent and sex ratio was 842. The Kanpur urban agglomeration had a population of 2,920,067 with a literacy rate of 83.98% and a sex ratio of 842.There are 35 Parsis in Kanpur with their Fire Temple at The Mall.Kanpur is majority Hindu with sizeable minorities of Buddhist, Sikh and Muslims.
